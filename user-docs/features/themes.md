# App Themes

Settings → Appearance ships **eight app themes**. Your choice repaints the whole app — brand chrome, accents, and the chat background all follow it, not just the top bar. Light/Dark/Auto applies to themes that ship both modes; fixed-mode themes show their own complete look.
