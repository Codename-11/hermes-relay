v1.2.6 — Tidier chats & calmer status.

• Chats no longer get stuck on "Untitled" — your first message stands in as the title until the chat is named, plus a new refresh button in the session list. Renaming a chat now sticks on non-default profiles.
• Connection status now slides in as a thin banner at the top instead of a card floating over your chat; the floating alert is kept for persistent errors.
